Redefining Business with Artificial Intelligence

Formerly known as NLP4Business in the two first editions, the 2026 edition of AI4BUSINESS seminar marks a fundamental shift. We are transcending text to enter the era of Multimodal AI where the convergence of language, image, and data dictates the future of corporate strategy. We aren't just discussing models; we are architecting the Industrialization of AI.
Join us as we move beyond the laboratory to build robust, containerized, and cost-efficient systems that deliver measurable impact in the heavy-hitting worlds of industies like Energy and Finance.
